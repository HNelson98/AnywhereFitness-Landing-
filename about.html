About

Here at Anywhere Fitness we understand that the world is changing, and fast. Hostion a workout at a studio, in a park, or even in a basment gym is becoming more and more frequent. With that in mind we set out to make it painless for Instructors and Clients alike to hold and attend Fitness classes wherever they might be held. We strive daily to improve this, so thank you for joining us on this journey!
